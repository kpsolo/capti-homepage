Capti Voice

Capti Voice is a universally accessible literacy tool enabling teachers to support, monitor, and assess students with diverse needs and abilities within a single classroom. Capti Voice empowers students to improve vocabulary, keep up with reading, and develop active reading strategies.

Capti Voice is universally accessible in that it works across user devices, content sources, content formats, student abilities, age groups, instructional goals, and subject areas. Capti makes it very easy to create playlists with textual content and have them narrated by high quality text-to-speech.

Capti has a number of innovative ML/AI/NLP-powered features including adaptive vocabulary games, contextual translation, language detection, content extraction, etc.
