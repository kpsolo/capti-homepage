Teachers face a multitude of challenges in the classroom. Among them is the excessive effort and time it takes to differentiate for students of different needs and reading levels.

Capti Voice is a literacy support tool that helps teachers personalize reading and learning for all students, while saving the teachers’ valuable time. Capti helps students overcome their learning difficulties, print disabilities, and language barriers.
